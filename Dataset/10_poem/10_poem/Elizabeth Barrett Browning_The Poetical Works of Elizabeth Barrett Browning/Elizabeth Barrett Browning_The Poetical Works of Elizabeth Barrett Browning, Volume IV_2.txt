Author: Elizabeth Barrett Browning
Title: The Poetical Works of Elizabeth Barrett Browning, Volume IV

But now ... God sees me, God, who took my heart And drowned it in life's surge. In all your wide warm earth I have no part-- A light song overcomes me like a dirge. Could Love's great harmony The saints keep step to when their bonds are loose, Not weigh me down? am _I_ a wife to choose? Look in my face and see--
