Author: Elizabeth Barrett Browning
Title: The Poetical Works of Elizabeth Barrett Browning, Volume IV

I think I hear him, how he cried "My own soul's life!" between their notes. Each man has but one soul supplied, And that's immortal. Though his throat's On fire with passion now, to _her_ He can't say what to me he said! And yet he moves her, they aver. The nightingales sing through my head,-- The nightingales, the nightingales!
