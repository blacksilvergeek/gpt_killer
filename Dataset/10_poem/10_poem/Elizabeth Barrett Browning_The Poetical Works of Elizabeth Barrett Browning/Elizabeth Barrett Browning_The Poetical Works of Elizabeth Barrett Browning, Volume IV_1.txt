Author: Elizabeth Barrett Browning
Title: The Poetical Works of Elizabeth Barrett Browning, Volume IV

I might have loved thee in some former days. Oh, then, my spirits had leapt As now they sink, at hearing thy love-praise! Before these faded cheeks were overwept, Had this been asked of me, To love thee with my whole strong heart and head,-- I should have said still ... yes, but _smiled_ and said, "Look in my face and see!"
