Author: Elizabeth Barrett Browning
Title: The Poetical Works of Elizabeth Barrett Browning, Volume IV

But set a springe for _him_, "mio ben," My only good, my first last love!-- Though Christ knows well what sin is, when He sees some things done they must move Himself to wonder. Let her pass. I think of her by night and day. Must _I_ too join her ... out, alas!... With Giulio, in each word I say? And evermore the nightingales!
