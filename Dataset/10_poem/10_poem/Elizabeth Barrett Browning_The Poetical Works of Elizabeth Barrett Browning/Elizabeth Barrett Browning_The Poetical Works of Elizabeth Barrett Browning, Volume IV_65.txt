Author: Elizabeth Barrett Browning
Title: The Poetical Works of Elizabeth Barrett Browning, Volume IV

You'll take back your Grand-duke? There _are_ some things to object to. He cheated, betrayed, and forsook, Then called in the foe to protect you. He taxed you for wines and for meats Throughout that eight years' pastime Of Austria's drum in your streets-- Of course you remember the last time You called back your Grand-duke?
