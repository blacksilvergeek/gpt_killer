Author: Elizabeth Barrett Browning
Title: The Poetical Works of Elizabeth Barrett Browning, Volume IV

You'll take back your Grand-duke? His son rode fast as he got off That day on the enemy's hook, When _I_ had an epaulette shot off. Though splashed (as I saw him afar--no Near) by those ghastly rains, The mark, when you've washed him in Arno, Will scarcely be larger than Cain's. You'll call back the Grand-duke?
