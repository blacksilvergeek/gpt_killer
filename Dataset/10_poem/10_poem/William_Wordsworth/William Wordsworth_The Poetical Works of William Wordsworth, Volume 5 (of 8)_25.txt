Author: William Wordsworth
Title: The Poetical Works of William Wordsworth, Volume 5 (of 8)

Ye have left my cell,--but see How Nature hems you in with friendly arms! And by her help ye are my prisoners still. But which way shall I lead you?--how contrive, In spot so parsimoniously endowed, That the brief hours, which yet remain, may reap Some recompense of knowledge or delight?
