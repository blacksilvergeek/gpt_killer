Author: Lord Byron
Title: The Works of Lord Byron, Volume 6

Upon what principle have you omitted ... one of the concluding stanzas sent as an addition?--because it ended, I suppose, with-- 'And do not link two virtuous souls for life Into that moral centaur, man and wife?' Now, I must say, once for all, that I will not permit any human being to take such liberties with my writings because I am absent. I desire the omissions to be replaced (except the stanza on Semiramis)--particularly the stanza upon the Turkish marriages
